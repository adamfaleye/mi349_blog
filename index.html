Bio

Ademola Faleye is an American football player who currently plays as a tight end for the Michigan State Spartans. Faleye was born in Brockton, Massachusetts and attended Brockton High School before playing for the Norfolk State Spartans during his freshman and sophomore years. Faleye entered the transfer portal after sophomore season, and received many offers from great football programs. Faleye committed to Michigan State and enrolled in January 2023, and participated in the team's spring activities. Ademola Faleye is known for his size, speed, strength, and agility on the football field, consistently making significant contributions to his team's success on both blocking and pass-catching situations. He is also recognized for his positive attitude and leadership qualities, inspiring and motivating his teammates to perform at their best.
